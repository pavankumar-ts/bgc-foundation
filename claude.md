# BGC Foundation Brand Context

## What This Website Is About

**BGC Foundation** is a non-profit healthcare foundation that brings specialized digestive health programs to rural communities in Karnataka, India.

## Brand Identity
- **Organization**: BGC Foundation
- **Mission**: Rural Healthcare for Digestive Health
- **Type**: Non-profit healthcare foundation
- **Language**: Use "programs" not "services" throughout

## Brand Colors
- refer tailwind config files

## Core Concept
They operate a "Hospital on Wheels" model - bringing specialist doctors and medical equipment directly to rural villages where people can't access urban hospitals.

## Key Leadership
**Dr. Yogananda Reddy** - Chief Gastroenterologist with international credentials leading the rural outreach efforts.

## Main Focus Areas
- Rural health camps with specialist consultations
- Mobile endoscopy procedures
- Health education in local language (Kannada)
- Seeking funding for a Mobile Endoscopy Unit (ambulance with built-in medical equipment)

## Tone & Approach
Professional medical foundation with compassionate, community-focused mission. Healthcare expertise meeting rural accessibility needs.
